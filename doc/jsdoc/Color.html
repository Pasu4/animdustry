Mixes two colors together.

amount number The amount of the second color
mode string <optional>
 "mix" The mixing mode. - mix: Interpolate between the colors. - add: Add the two colors. - sub: Subtract `col2` from `col1`. - mul: Multiply the two colors. - div: Divide `col1` by `col2`. - and: Bitwise AND. - or: Bitwise OR. - xor: Bitwise XOR. - not: Bitwise NOT on `col1`.
